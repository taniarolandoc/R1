Restaurant Campaigns

Discover exciting restaurant ventures seeking funding. Each campaign provides comprehensive details about the restaurant's vision, business model, and growth strategy.

  • Detailed business plans and financial projections
  • Funding goals with transparent use of funds breakdown
  • Restaurant photos, menus, and concept descriptions
  • Risk assessments and market analysis
  • Owner background and experience
  • Timeline and milestone roadmap

Track Your Investments

Stay informed with comprehensive investment tracking tools. Monitor your portfolio performance, track returns, and make data-driven decisions.

  • Real-time portfolio dashboard with performance metrics
  • Investment history and transaction logs
  • ROI calculator and returns projections
  • Diversification analysis across restaurants
  • Performance charts and visualizations
  • Email alerts for important updates

Milestone Tracking

Ensure accountability with milestone-based fund disbursement. Restaurants must achieve specific goals before accessing the next round of funding.

  • Clear milestone definitions and requirements
  • Progress updates with photo and document evidence
  • Staged fund release tied to milestone completion
  • Investor voting on milestone achievement
  • Timeline tracking and deadline management
  • Automated notifications for milestone updates

Blockchain Powered

Built on blockchain technology for maximum transparency and security. Every transaction is recorded immutably, creating an auditable trail of all fund movements.

  • Immutable transaction records on the blockchain
  • Smart contracts for automated fund release
  • Transparent capital usage tracking
  • Verify any transaction independently
  • Decentralized trust without intermediaries
  • Tamper-proof audit trail for compliance

Verified Restaurants

Every restaurant undergoes rigorous verification before launching a campaign. We validate business legitimacy, financial health, and operational capability.

  • Business registration and license verification
  • Financial health checks and credit assessment
  • Operational history and track record review
  • Owner background checks and KYC
  • Location and premises verification
  • Ongoing compliance monitoring

Community Driven

Join a vibrant community of food lovers and local business supporters. Share experiences, read reviews, and connect with fellow investors and restaurant owners.

  • Investor reviews and ratings for funded restaurants
  • Success stories and case studies
  • Community forums and discussions
  • Restaurant updates and news feed
  • Networking events and meetups
  • Exclusive perks and rewards for active members
